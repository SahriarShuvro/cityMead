On-Site Services
Ananta Terraces offers high-quality on-site services which ensure a stress-free and balanced lifestyle.

1. Synthetic Professional Jogging Tracks

A 4-lane track that runs for 840m meters across the entire property, equipped with outdoor speakers.

2. Squash Courts

Equipped with soft padding, 22mm solid Beech Sylva Squash wooden floors, armor coat plasters on the walls, and Perry Ellison glass on the back wall. Squash Markers will be available at all times for training.

3. Tennis Court

Professional hard-coat court, with an acrylic surface preparation to guarantee the best training experience. On-site coach available to train residents.

4. Multipurpose Courts

Synthetic courts for basketball, handball, and other games. Properly fenced and illuminated for optimum playing experience at night. The courts will be monitored by professional coaches.
