Our Team

MD. AMINUR RASHID
CHAIRMAN
Edison Real Estate has the bold vision of making high-quality and nicely designed residences that are available within the purchasing power of a wider segment of the population. We clearly understand the need for more trusted and reliable companies in the real estate sector.

Companies will fill up this vacuum in the market and there will be a continuous effort to delight customers. We are a competent and motivated group of people, suppliers, and partners who will work closely to ensure strict processes and policies are followed in order to provide complete and consistent customer values. We will listen to our customers to drive continuous improvement and serve them with premium service in every way possible.

AHMED-PASHA
Director
Ahmed Pasha finished both his Honors and Masters in Economics from the University of Dhaka and an additional MBA under the prestigious IBA (University of Dhaka).

Mr. Pasha has a diverse portfolio with over 20 years of professional experience. He has closely worked for Brand Development and Marketing activities for various local and international brands.

Subsequently, he has significantly contributed his knowledge and expertise with Nielsen Bangladesh, in addition to over 50 other research projects. After gaining adequate knowledge on consumer behavior, he joined SIEMENS Bangladesh to implement his learning where he led a business unit of OSRAM for 3 consecutive years.

Mr. Pasha has been a part of Edison Group since the very early days of the company. As a member of the top management board, he actively participated in developing a company strategy, policy, growth process and human development. Mr. Pasha has now taken up the challenge of making Edison Real Estate the most caring Real Estate brand in Bangladesh, as a board member.

SHAEDUL-KARIM-MUNNA
Additional Director
Sahedul Karim Munna, is an experienced management professional. He has completed his Bachelors and Masters in Management. He has a diverse portfolio with over 20 years of experience as a management professional. Before joining Edison Real Estate Limited, he served in many industries with several important roles and showed his expertise in managing critical functions such as operation, business expansion, investment appraisal, auditing, reporting and managing employees.

He has an impressive history of surpassing business goals and creating revolutionary changes in all operational areas through his visualization and prudence within the company. Besides, he has a consistent record of positioning the organization towards success and building highly efficient and dedicated teams that demonstrate operational excellence.

At Edison Real Estate Limited, he is currently serving as the Chief Operating Officer and responsible for managing the overall business operations of the company. Under his leadership, Edison Real Estate Limited has become a fully integrated and exhaustive business organization.

MD-FAKHRUL-ISLAM
Advisor
Md. Fakhrul Islam has over 37 years of glorious career as a banking management professional. He served in the banking industry and played important roles and showed his expertise in managing critical functions.

Md. Islam started his career back in July 1984 at BIDS (Bangladesh Institute of Development Studies) as Research Officer. He served American Express Bank Ltd. as Manager - Trade Finance for 14 years. Prior to joining Edison Real Estate Ltd., he retired from Dhaka Bank as Executive Vice President where he was Head of Operations & AMLD for 20 years.

Like a guardian angel, Md. Fakhrul overlooks the corporation’s integral activities so that our promises and deliverables parallel with our vision and values.

Management Board

aminur-rashid

Chairman

Aminur Rashid finished his graduation under the prestigious IBA, The University Of Dhaka. He has 25 years of corporate and entrepreneurial experience and expertise under his belt. Having started his career in Siemens Bangladesh, he became the Director of Industry & Consumer Products Division of SIEMENS Bangladesh Ltd. within only 6 years.

After his success at SIEMENS, he had his heart set on a new venture. With the help of his team, he started the brand, SYMPHONY back in 2008.

Within just a few years it became the highest-selling cell phone brand in Bangladesh, holding around 45% of the total market share. This extraordinary series of achievements made him the great leader he is today. He started Edison Real Estate with the vision of building the most trusted real estate brand in Bangladesh.
