Construction at the Heart of the Ananta-Ascentis Partnership

Construction of the Ananta Terraces project is handled by Ananta Real Estate, a team of highly-trained engineers with many years of global construction experience, all of which is reflected in a built environment portfolio spanning 250,000 square feet.

Ascentis Group is the in charge of the project timeline, quality and the overall construction management. The group's technical expertise is unrivaled, securing value at every stage of the project's development and higher returns upon project completion.

Collaborating with a team of industry-leading experts, we ensure that Ananta Terraces epitomizes luxury and sustainability. The Principal Architect of the project is Calvin Sim, from the world renowned architectural firm, Ecoid. Calvin, with his team, and primarily with the famous Japanese architect, Seijiro Hayashi, created the breathtaking spaces at Ananta Terraces. Features inspired by iconic buildings in New York. Large terrace spaces and green landscaping inside every apartment. The building is designed with a complete white façade and the green-tinted, low E double glazed glass adds a modern touch to the architectural finish.

The Interior Architect of the project is Carol, also from Ecoid; a firm with a huge amount of experience in creating diverse, world-class commercial and residential interior spaces for some of the world’s best known corporate and real estate brands, using pioneering technology and global design methodologies.

WME (www.wmeglobal.com) is an independent, multi-disciplinary consultancy, providing engineering services for the built environment. Based in Dubai, Abu Dhabi, London, Riyadh, Singapore, Bangalore, and Mumbai, they offer engineering solutions around the globe.

Their primary services include Structural Engineering, Building Services/MEP, AV/IT/Security, Roads & Infrastructure, and Lead Design Consultancy.

WME has undertaken design engineering on a number of prestigious projects, including: UAE Pavilion, Expo2020, Dubai; Downtown Views, Nakheel Mall & Tower [St Regis Hotel] on palm Jumeirah; SkyCity Borivali, Mumbai; Arabian Ranches Phase III, Dubai; Exit 10, Riyadh, and many more.

WME engineers are passionate about creative buildability and deliver practical, cost-effective design solutions through an in-depth knowledge of best practices and an openness to innovation at all stages of a project's development, oftentimes in the most demanding climates. Their mission is to engineer, supervise, and enhance developments from the inside-out in support of a diversity of assets. WME specializes in projects that focus on performance, economics, and sustainability, which translates into giving life to buildings without compromising the envirionment.

Prof. Dr. M. Shamim Z. Bosunia is a prominent academic, leader and structural engineering expert from Bangladesh. The areas of his work include the East-West Interconnector Transmission Line over Jamuna, a 500-bed hospital in Bogra, the repair and rehabilitation of hundreds of cyclone shelters in the coastal areas of Bangladesh, the preparation of a master plan for cyclone shelters, the repair and strengthening of Banglabandhu National Stadium and Mirpur Cricket Stadium, the design of a number of high-rise buildings in the country, including the 39-storied City Center in Dhaka, the design of Mirpur Indoor Stadium, and the design of various bridges of RHD.
