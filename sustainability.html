We use double glazed low E glass in all windows to improve the efficient use of our solar energy supply. We provide a non-fossil fuel facility for vehicles and we work with eco-friendly housekeeping chemicals, monitoring and maintaining our high standards of sustainable living through regular certifications. Ananta Terraces is an opportunity to soar toward unparalleled, organic, green harmony, both indoors and out.

The Solar Powered Led Smart Street Light Poles, installed throughout the Ananta Terraces community, save power, record temperatures, and provide information on humidity, on air quality, and on which WIFI carriers are nearby, all through the use of easy-to-use smart touchscreens.

At Ananta, we dedicate a large amount of the budget to sustainable living features so that our residents live in a place that takes care of their wellbeing, on all levels, every single day.
