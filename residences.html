Common Features

Architecture, Structure and MEP design by renowned international firms to ensure modern integrated engineering solutions that focuses on performance, economics, and sustainability.

All electrical system is designed to ensure international human safety norms. Access to all the amenities/club and common facilities. Addressable Intelligent type Fire Alarm and Detection system for the apartment. 24/7 uninterrupted treated water supply design on WHO guideline for compliance with domestic water use.

All apartments and habitable areas are provided with smoke detector and heat sensors. All apartments, security rooms, building reception, maintenance room and IBMS rooms are integrated with EPBAX system. All services shall be controlled and monitored through an integrated building management system (IBMS) for greater reliability and to effect optimum level of operation of engineering services systems. Durable and corrosion resistant concealed plumbing works utilizing CPVC food grade pipes. EV Charging stations will be provided in the parking area to cater to the need of the Electric Vehicles. Dedicated Energy Meters for DESCO direct supply and for 100% power Back-up electrical diesel Generator. Central UPS emergency backup system is designed for fire alarming system, emergency lights, CCTV &security system, BMS system.
